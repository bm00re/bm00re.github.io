Sri Santhanam
Best Man

Sri graduated a year after me from the University of Illinois MBA program. While we worked in a student-run consulting firm together for a semester, our friendship blossomed once Sri relocated to Dallas after school to work for AT&T.

Sri is the life of the party. His second home is It'll Do, a small EDM club in Deep Ellum. Never short on quirky intellectual conversation and thoughtful encouragement, Sri's a good dude.

Abby Craig
Maid of Honor

Abby, soon to be Mrs. Cousert, is my sister from another mister -- the sushi to my pho. On a fateful day in the Midwest, we met at a Latin dancing social. We hit it off with our shared passion for rhythm. But our bond was forged through our shared values and small-town adventures. We even shared a roof for 3 years until I moved to Dallas. Before my eyes, I've seen Abby's work ethic lead her to recently becoming a leasing manager at one of the largest property management firms in Champaign-Urbana.

Oh, and did I mention that she has 21 siblings?

Gonenc Mete
Groomsman

Mete, as he goes, was one of my first friends at the University of Illinois MBA program. We spent many-a-night at his apartment, sipping fine whipped cream vodka, as we talked of what our future might be. Eventually, Mete had a hair-brained scheme and we worked (with McCay to boot) on a startup together tirelessly for the last semester of school.

Now, Mete is half-a-world-away in the Valley of Silicon chips and nerdy dreams. We've had yearly re-encounters but distance certainly makes the heart grow fonder. He's a big shot now, having the honor to work along brilliant minds at the ever-desirable Facebook, each day making the world a better digital place.

Groomsman

Patty Qi
Bridesmaid

Patty is a girl of many interests. Of all my friends, she is the foremost foodie and quite the world-traveler.

I had the pleasure of meeting Patty in Greece and Turkey as part of the study abroad program at the University of Illinois, where she studied to be a CPA. She hosted me in her home country of China (where she is living now) and it was one of the most intimate and memorable trips I've ever had.
